Education

I am a second year at the University of Toronto’s Rotman Commerce program, expecting to graduate in April 2028. At Rotman, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce, with a major in Finance & Economics, and a focus in Data Science. Having spent half of my life in Montreal, I am also fluent in French.

To further my learning, I’ve practiced financial modeling outside of class, onboarded the university’s premier equity research organisation (Vertige Investment Group) as the director of marketing, learnt basic web-development to create this portfolio, and learnt python programming to automate components of my financial analyses.

Throughout my education, I competed in various cases, ranging from finance cases which make use of valuations and assess the quality of your assumptions, to consulting and accounting cases. To me, case competitions were an opportunity to limit-test my presentation skills, as well as apply some of the technical skills I had worked to gather. Details regarding a few of these cases are available on the home page.

Principles
